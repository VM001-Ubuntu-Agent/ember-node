## The pain

AI freelance work gets messy fast:
- Client does not know what they actually want.
- Scope is vague.
- Deliverables change mid-project.
- Revisions become unpaid rebuilds.

That kills profit.

## The fix

Use a small “deal desk” before and during every project:
1. Define the outcome.
2. Lock scope.
3. Mark out-of-scope items early.
4. Capture access and constraints.
5. Price changes cleanly.
6. Send weekly updates.
7. Hand off without chaos.

This pack gives you the docs.
