# Risk Register Template (Freelancer Projects)

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|-------|
| Missing API access | Medium | High | Access checklist before start | Client |
| Scope creep | High | High | Change-order rule | Freelancer |
| Slow stakeholder feedback | Medium | Medium | Weekly deadline reminders | Both |
| Bad source data | Medium | High | Data validation gate | Client |
