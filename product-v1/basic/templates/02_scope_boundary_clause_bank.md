# Scope Boundary Clause Bank

Use copy/paste clauses:

1. Scope freeze clause:
"Any request outside the defined deliverables is treated as a change request and priced separately."

2. Revision cap clause:
"This project includes [X] revision rounds. Additional rounds billed at £[rate]."

3. Third-party delay clause:
"Delays from third-party tools, APIs, or client access provision may shift timelines."

4. Data quality clause:
"Output quality depends on source data quality and access permissions provided by client."

5. Handoff boundary:
"Post-handoff support beyond [X] days is a separate support retainer."
