# STRATEGY OVERVIEW (ANALYTICAL)

## Objective
Build reliable income engine with low daily involvement.

## Income path (v1)
Traffic -> Landing Page -> Email Capture -> Offer -> Checkout -> Delivery -> Upsell

## Success metrics (minimum)
- Visitors/day
- Email opt-in rate
- Sales conversion rate
- Revenue/day

## £50/day math examples
- 3 sales/day at £19 = £57/day
- 2 sales/day at £39 = £78/day
- 6 sales/day at £9 = £54/day

## Phase plan
Phase 1: First sale proof
Phase 2: Repeatable daily traffic
Phase 3: Automation + upsells
Phase 4: Multi-product scaling

## Risk controls
- No paid ads until offer converts organically.
- Keep costs near zero early.
- Validate demand before huge build.

## Automation later
- Scheduled content posting support
- Email sequence automation
- Lead tracking dashboard
- Weekly optimization loop
