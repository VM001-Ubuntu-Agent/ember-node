# Fast Launch Loop - 7 Days

Purpose: get first sale proof fast.

Do not overthink.
Post. Talk. Track. Improve.

## Human setup needed first

You must create the sell page because payout/KYC needs you.

Fast path:
1. Open Gumroad or Payhip.
2. Create product: AI Freelancer Deal Desk Starter.
3. Upload ZIP from `dist/`.
4. Add 3 tiers: £9 / £19 / £39.
5. Paste copy from `launch-assets/landing-page/LANDING_PAGE_V2.md`.
6. Send Hermes the live links.

## Daily loop, 45 minutes

### Minute 0-5: Check numbers
Open `launch-assets/tracking/FIRST_7_DAYS_TRACKER.csv`.
Fill yesterday/today numbers.

### Minute 5-20: Post 3 times
Use posts from:
`launch-assets/social-posts/LAUNCH_POSTS_10.md`

Post one of each:
- Pain post: scope creep story
- Useful post: one clause/template tip
- Soft offer post: link to product

### Minute 20-35: Comment/help
Find AI freelancer / automation posts.
Leave 10 useful comments.
No spam.
Give one real tip.

### Minute 35-45: DM warm people
Only DM people who interacted or asked about client/project problems.
Use this:

"Hey, saw your comment about AI client scope getting messy. I made a small template pack for AI freelancers to prevent scope creep. Want the link? No pressure."

If they say yes, send product link.

## Day-by-day focus

### Day 1
Publish product. Post announcement. Ask 5 people for feedback.

### Day 2
Post scope creep examples. Push Pro tier.

### Day 3
Post before/after proposal sections.

### Day 4
Post change-order example. This is the money pain.

### Day 5
Post onboarding checklist. Ask audience what docs they use.

### Day 6
Post mini case study: "one unpaid change can cost more than this pack".

### Day 7
Review metrics. Keep, tweak, or pivot.

## Red flags

Do not spend days polishing.
Do not build new product before traffic test.
Do not buy ads yet.
Do not spam Reddit/DMs.

## Success target

First proof target:
- 150 targeted visitors
- 1-3 sales
- at least 3 useful buyer/lead objections captured
