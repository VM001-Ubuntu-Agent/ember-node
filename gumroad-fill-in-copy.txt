DESCRIPTION
ScopeGuard Client Docs Generator is a simple browser app for AI and automation freelancers.

It helps freelancers avoid scope creep and unpaid extra work by turning messy client/project details into clear client-ready documents.

The buyer fills in a simple form and clicks Generate. The app creates:
- proposal text
- scope boundary clauses
- onboarding checklist
- change-order message
- weekly client update

It runs locally in the browser.
No login.
No server.
No setup.
No subscription.

The buyer downloads the ZIP, opens index.html, fills the form, and copies or downloads the generated client docs.
